The capital and largest city in Greece is Athens which is where the Parthenon is located in the Acropolis.

Greece is the birth place of democracy, the Olympic games

Greece is located in the Mediterranean Sea. The Greek coastline borders the Ionian Sea and the Aegean Sea as well as the Libyan and Crete Seas in the south.

Greece also includes an extensive group of islands of about 6 000 islands with Crete being the largest island.

Athens is known as the oldest capital city in Europe. Athens is named after Athena, the Greek goddess of wisdom.

Greece's highest mountain is Mount Olympus.
