Hej Verden! (Hello World!)

This page focuses on Denmark with several intersting key facts and intriguing pictures.

Denmark is a Western European country that shares its only land border with Germany. Denmark also borders the Baltic and North seas, and the Öresund bridge links it to nearby Sweden. Let’s explore some cool facts about Denmark.
